Chapter One

The memories we keep close

Small moments that became big ones. Moments that will stay in our hearts

  1. The river

    Feet in the water, hearts wide open

    That afternoon by the river, laughter louder than the current, the whole family together.

  2. The departure

    Before the road

    Three of us posed in front of the white car. The kind of picture that says we were here, together, before we drove off into another good day.

  3. Arras, France

    An ordinary table, an unforgettable evening

    A simple meal, the kids around. Mama at the center, like always.

  4. Paris

    Cobblestones, lace and the little one

    Mama and Chloé in the streets of Paris — two generations holding the same smile.

  5. The apple tree

    Three of us, and a fourth on the way

    Under the apple tree — the day we knew another little soul was coming to join the family. Lollipop on the way.

  6. Golden hour

    Mother and son

    A walk, the sun going to sleep behind us — a moment you can almost hear when you look at it.
